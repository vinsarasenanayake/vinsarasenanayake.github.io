Welcome to Serenity Health Care

Welcome to Serenity Health Care, where we blend compassionate care with advanced medical services. Our facility offers a wide range of services, including emergency care, cardiology, orthopedics, and oncology. With a dedicated team and state-of-the-art technology, we provide personalized treatment and support for all your healthcare needs. At Serenity Health Care, your well-being is our top priority.

FACILITIES



Integrated Wellness Center: The new facility includes an integrated wellness center offering holistic health services such as nutrition counseling, mental health support, and preventive care programs. This center aims to promote overall well-being and support a healthier lifestyle for our patients.


Rehabilitation and Therapy Center: Our updated facility features an advanced rehabilitation and therapy center with cutting-edge equipment and therapy rooms. This center supports comprehensive physical and occupational therapy, helping patients recover and regain their independence in a supportive environment.
