Serenity Health Care Family

Colombo Branch (Main Branch)

Serenity Health Care – Colombo Branch

Located in the heart of Colombo, our main branch is equipped with state-of-the-art medical facilities and a dedicated team of healthcare professionals. Offering a comprehensive range of services, including emergency care, outpatient surgery, and advanced diagnostic imaging, this branch serves as the hub of our healthcare network. Our Colombo branch is committed to providing exceptional care in a comfortable and modern environment.

Galle Branch

Serenity Health Care – Galle Branch

Our Galle branch is a well-established facility that provides high-quality healthcare services to the southern region. With specialized departments in diagnostic imaging and laboratory services, we cater to both routine and urgent medical needs. The Galle branch prides itself on personalized care and its strong connection to the local community, ensuring accessible and effective healthcare solutions.

Batticalo Branch

Serenity Health Care – Batticaloa Branch

The Batticaloa branch is dedicated to delivering excellent medical care in the eastern part of Sri Lanka. Known for its welcoming atmosphere and professional medical staff, this branch focuses on outpatient care and rehabilitation services. The Batticaloa branch is designed to offer convenient and efficient healthcare services tailored to the needs of the local population.
